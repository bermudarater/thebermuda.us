About Us

The Infrastructure Layer for Insurance

Bermuda is a Dallas-based InsurTech company with 10+ years of specialization in the insurance domain. We believe technology should simplify, not complicate — so we build the infrastructure agencies run on, not another dashboard to manage.

Our platform spans three products that work together: the Rater (lead-to-quote-to-bind automation), Carrier Downloads (live carrier-portal data and payment automation), and Agentic AI. Bermuda connects your rater, CRM, AMS, and carrier portals into digital, automated workflows.

And as the industry shifts to AI, we believe an agent without tools and live data just talks. That's why everything we build — real-time carrier data, quoting, binding, payments — doubles as the toolset AI agents need to do real work for your agency.

Our commitment is to focus on what matters most: Your success!

Frequently Asked Questions

Bermuda Rater is a powerful rating engine and integration platform designed for insurance carriers, MGAs, and agencies. It enables real-time quote generation, seamless API connections with carriers and CRMs, and automated workflows to power your customer portals and AMS.

Bermuda focuses on enterprise insurance agencies — operations with custom workflows, multiple data sources, and high policy volume. We also serve carriers, MGAs, and digital agencies building insurance technology, with integrations for rating APIs, CRMs, conversion tools, and document systems.

Yes — Bermuda is agent-agnostic. Our platform is exposed as MCP (Model Context Protocol) tool servers, so Claude, ChatGPT, or your custom agent connects natively to quoting, policy data, payments, and carrier uploads — live data in, actions out. Access is OAuth-authenticated and default-deny: each agent uses only the tools you explicitly permit, with input-level guardrails like payment caps.

Every agent authenticates via OAuth and is governed by a default-deny policy engine: it can only call the tools you explicitly permit. Policies go down to input level — for example, capping the payment amount an agent can process — and every tool call is logged and monitored.

IVANS is batch — downloads are hours old by the time they reach your AMS. Carrier Downloads reads due amounts, due dates, late fees, and statuses live from carrier portals at the moment of action — which is what makes payment and cancellation automation safe to run.

Pricing is custom — scoped to your agency's workflows, carriers, integrations, and volume. There's no fixed plan list. Contact sales and we'll scope it with you.
